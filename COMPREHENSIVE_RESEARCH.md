- Fixed costs: $2,500/month
- Average profit: $3.76/order

- Unbatched 2 orders: $13.60 driver pay
- Batched 2 orders: $7.40 driver pay
- **Savings: $6.20 (46%)**
- Monthly savings at 20 batched orders/day: **$1,740**

## 2. DRIVER PAY & RETENTION

### Prop 22 Requirements (California)
| Component | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Minimum Pay | $19.80/hr | 120% of $16.50 state min |
| Mileage | $0.36/mile | True-up minimum |
| Your Pay Rate | $5 base + $0.60/mile | Above minimum |
| Healthcare | $5/hr | After 15 hrs/week |

### Driver Retention Research
**Industry Stats:**
- DoorDash driver turnover: **80-100% annually**
- Top reasons drivers quit:
  1. Low pay (not meeting $20/hr expectation)
  2. Long wait times at restaurants
  3. Lack of transparency
  4. Poor treatment by customers/restaurants
  5. Car wear and tear costs

**Retention Strategies:**
1. **Pay transparency** - Show full earnings breakdown
2. **Fast payouts** - Daily or instant (vs weekly)
3. **Recognition** - "Driver of the Week" bonuses
4. **Communication** - Direct line to support
5. **Equipment** - Provide insulated bags, phone mounts
6. **Fuel discounts** - Partner with gas stations
7. **Insurance help** - Rideshare coverage assistance

**Paradise Delivery Advantages:**
- Higher base pay ($5 vs DoorDash $2-3)
- Better mileage ($0.60 vs $0.30)
- Smaller market = less competition
- Local support (not call center)
- Prop 22 compliance = guaranteed minimum

### Driver Incentives
| Incentive | Cost | Impact |
|-----------|------|--------|
| Sign-up bonus | $100 | Attracts new drivers |
| Weekly bonus (50+ orders) | $50 | Retention |
| Peak pay (+$2/delivery) | Variable | Coverage during rush |
| Quality bonus (4.8+ rating) | $25/week | Better service |
| Referral bonus | $100 | Network growth |

## 5. FRAUD PREVENTION & REFUNDS

### Industry Fraud Rates
- **Refund rate**: 3% of orders (30 per 1,000)
- **Chargeback rate**: 1.5% of revenue
- **"Friendly fraud"**: 22-42% of refunds (customer lies)
- **Driver fraud**: <1% (theft, fake delivery)

### Paradise Delivery System
**Risk Scoring (0-100):**
- 0-25: Auto-approve full refund
- 26-50: Auto-approve partial (50%)
- 51-70: Manual review
- 71-85: Decline + offer $5 credit
- 86-100: Decline + flag account

**Fraud Prevention:**
1. Photo required for "Leave at Door"
2. GPS tracking of drivers
3. Customer refund rate monitoring
4. Pattern detection (same reason repeatedly)
5. ID verification for high-value orders

**Cost:** $1.35/order (3.2% refund rate + reserves)

## 10. COMPETITION ANALYSIS

### DoorDash (Primary Competitor)
**Strengths:**
- Brand recognition
- Large driver pool
- Nationwide coverage
- $10B+ war chest

**Weaknesses:**
- High fees (30% commission)
- Impersonal (call centers)
- Slow restaurant payouts
- Unfair refund policies
- Treats drivers poorly

**Paradise Advantage:** Local, personal, fair pricing

### UberEats
**Strengths:**
- Uber driver pool (shared)
- Brand recognition

**Weaknesses:**
- Expensive fees
- Limited Paradise coverage
- Poor restaurant support

### Grubhub
**Declining market share**
- Being acquired/consolidated
- Not a major threat in small markets

### Local Competition
- **None currently** in Paradise
- Opportunity: First-mover advantage
- Risk: DoorDash could lower fees to compete

### Competitive Response Strategy
**If DoorDash lowers to 20%:**
- Match commission (18% → 15%)
- Emphasize: "Local, not corporate"
- Double down on personal service
- Loyalty programs

**Advantages you keep:**
- Local ownership
- Personal relationships
- Community focus
- Better driver pay
- Fairer refund system
