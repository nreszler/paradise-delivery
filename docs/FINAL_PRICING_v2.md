# Final Pricing Model v2.0

## Executive Summary

Paradise Delivery operates on a **radically transparent pricing model** designed to save customers 25-35% compared to competitors while maintaining sustainable unit economics.

**Core Philosophy:**
- Customers pay exactly what they see
- No hidden markups on menu items
- Fair compensation for all parties
- Fraud protection that doesn't punish honest customers

---

## Behind-the-Scenes Economics

### Revenue Distribution

For a $30 order with $3 service fee and $2.99 delivery fee:

```
Revenue Sources:
├── Service Fee (10%):          $3.00
├── Delivery Fee:               $2.99
│
Expense Allocation:
├── Driver Pay:                 $4.50 (base + mileage)
├── Payment Processing (2.9%):  $0.87
├── Customer Refund Reserve:    $0.30 (10% of service fee)
├── Platform Operations:        $0.20
├── Fraud/Dispute Costs:        $0.12
└── Net Profit:                 $0.00-0.50

Note: Profit comes from scale, not markup extraction
```

### Driver Compensation

| Component | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Base Pay | $2.50 | Per delivery |
| Per Mile | $0.65 | From pickup to dropoff |
| Wait Time | $0.15/min | After 5 min at restaurant |
| 100% of Tips | Variable | Customer sets 0-20% |

**Example Driver Earnings:**
- 3-mile delivery: $2.50 + (3 × $0.65) = $4.45 base
- Plus $4.50 tip (15% of $30) = **$8.95 total**

## Refund Economics

### Automatic Refund Budget

We allocate **10% of service fee revenue** to a refund reserve fund:

```
For every $1 in service fees:
├── Refund Reserve:     $0.10
├── Operations:         $0.15
├── Marketing:          $0.15
├── Tech/Infrastructure:$0.25
└── Profit:             $0.35 (at scale)
```

### Refund Thresholds

| Claim Type | Auto-Approve | Requires Review |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|
| Missing item (<$15) | Yes | No |
| Wrong item (<$20) | Yes | Account history check |
| Quality issue | Photo required | Yes, if >$25 |
| Late delivery | Automatic tracking | No |
| Multiple claims | No | Pattern analysis |

### Fraud Prevention Costs

- **AI Assessment**: $0.05 per refund claim
- **Manual Review**: $2.50 per escalated case
- **False Positive Rate Target**: <2%

## Unit Economics at Scale

### Break-Even Analysis

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 6 | Month 12 |
|--------|---------|---------|----------|
| Orders/Day | 100 | 500 | 2,000 |
| Avg Order Value | $28 | $32 | $35 |
| Revenue/Day | $299 | $1,745 | $7,980 |
| Gross Margin | -15% | 8% | 18% |
| CAC Recovery | 90 days | 45 days | 30 days |

### Key Assumptions

- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: $25
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: $450 (18-month retention)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 18:1 (healthy)
- **Monthly Churn**: 8% (industry avg: 15%)
