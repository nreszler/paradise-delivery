# Marketing Copy & Brand Guidelines

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Honest & Transparent**: No marketing fluff, straight facts
- **Friendly but Professional**: Approachable, not corporate
- **Empowering**: Customers make informed decisions
- **Trustworthy**: We protect our customers

### Words We Use
| ✅ Use | ❌ Avoid |
|--------|----------|
| Fair pricing | Cheap |
| Transparent | Hidden fees (ironic) |
| Honest | Free (when it's not) |
| Real cost | Surge pricing |
| Customer protection | Insurance (too corporate) |
| Fair pay | Tips subsidize wages |

## Key Messaging

### Headlines

**Primary:**
> "Finally, a delivery app that doesn't overcharge you."

**Secondary:**
> "See exactly what you pay for. Every time."
> "The delivery app that actually cares about your wallet."
> "No hidden fees. No markups. Just honest delivery."

### Value Propositions

**For Customers:**
1. **Save 25-35%** on every order vs competitors
2. **Know exactly** what you're paying for
3. **Automatic refunds** when things go wrong
4. **No surprise fees** at checkout

**For Restaurants:**
1. **Keep 100%** of menu prices
2. **Lower fees** than competitors
3. **Faster payouts** (daily vs weekly)
4. **Direct customer relationship**

**For Drivers:**
1. **100% of tips** go to you
2. **Fair base pay** for every delivery
3. **Transparent earnings** breakdown
4. **Instant pay** available
