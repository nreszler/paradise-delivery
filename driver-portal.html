📋 California Prop 22 Compliance

You're protected under California law. As an independent contractor with Paradise Delivery, you receive:

  • 120% of minimum wage for active delivery time ($5.00 base exceeds this)
  • $0.60 per mile reimbursement (67% above the $0.36 minimum)
  • Healthcare subsidy if you work 15+ hours per week
  • Occupational accident insurance coverage
  • No delivery minimums - work when you want

We pay more than required. Our $5 base + $0.60/mile exceeds Prop 22 minimums on every delivery. You'll never need a "true-up" payment - you already earn above the guarantee.
